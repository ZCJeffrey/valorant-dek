HOW TO PLAY

VALORANT

The aim is to become the first team to win 13 rounds. To accomplish this,
players must either eliminate the members of the opposing team
or finish the objective assigned to them.

For Attackers, this involves planting a spike (or a bomb).
They must plant and defend the spike against the opposing team
until it successfully goes off. The attacking side receives the point in the round
if the spike detonates or the group manages to eliminate the enemy team.

The Defenders are tasked to neutralize the bomb as quickly as possible.
This involves the players defusing it within a specific time.
The group receives the point if this attempt is successful.
